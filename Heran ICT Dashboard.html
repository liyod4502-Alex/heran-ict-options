A-Trade (4H+1H) — Full confluence. Highest win rate. Risk 3–5%.
4H bias + 4H HH/HL + 1H above SMA + 1H disp vol ≥1.5× + 1H FVG retest.

1
Pre-market — Set 4H bias (8:00–9:15 AM ET)
Open TradingView on 4H chart. Is price above/below 50 SMA? Is SMA sloping? Are you seeing HH/HL (bull) or LL/LH (bear)? This is your A-Trade direction for the day. If 4H is ranging — A-trades are off. B-trades remain available if 1H + 15M align.
The Pine Script dashboard shows all of this live — green/red on every check.
Before open
2
TradingView alert fires — A or B signal
Pine Script auto-checks all structural conditions and fires the correct alert:
BULL A / BEAR A — all 4H+1H checks passed. Highest priority.
BULL B / BEAR B — 1H+15M aligned, no 4H required. More frequent.
Alert message tells you which trade type and confirms which checks passed.
Alert driven
3
Switch to 15M — confirm entry candle
Drop to 15M chart. Wait for:
Bull: 15M candle that closes above midpoint inside FVG — volume ≥1.2× avg. Engulf or BOS preferred.
Bear: 15M rejection wick or bearish close showing sellers — same volume rule.
If first 15M candle is indecisive (doji/small body) — wait one more. Max 2–3 candles then skip.
On alert — max 3 candles
4
Run options checklist — Strikes tab + Market Chameleon
Go to Strikes tab → select ticker → fetch live price → Find Strikes. Select ideal delta range (0.50–0.63).
Open marketchameleon.com to confirm: IV rank (want 15–40), earnings date (avoid within 3–5 days), term structure.
On broker options chain verify: OI ≥500, tight bid/ask, theta ≤8%/day.
DTE rule: IV rank <20 → 10–14 DTE. IV rank 20–40 → 14–21 DTE.
Before placing order
5
Size the trade — Risk tab
Go to Risk tab. Enter the option ask price.
A-Trade: 3% risk standard ($270), 5% on high-conviction ($450).
B-Trade: 2% max ($180). B-trades are frequent — protect capital.
Calculator shows exact contracts, total cost, and all PT levels.
Before entry
6
Enter — set stop immediately
Enter at market or limit (1¢ above ask). Immediately set:
Hard stop: option premium drops 40% from entry — exit no questions asked.
Time stop: if no movement after 45–60 min, exit. Theta is working against you.
Entry + stops
7
Manage — tiered exits
PT1 +30–35%: Sell 40% — move stop to breakeven on rest.
PT2 +55–65%: Sell another 35% — now running 25% free.
Runner +80–100%+: Trail at -20% from premium high. Exit by 3:45 PM.
If PT1 fires and price pulls back to entry — close everything. Never give back a win.
During trade
8
Exit rules — time and structure
Close all same-day trades by 3:45 PM ET — spreads widen in last 15 min.
Friday rule: close 100% by 3:00 PM. No overnight, no exceptions.
Structure break: 1H candle closes through FVG midpoint against trade → exit immediately.
5 DTE rule: any runner reaching 5 DTE remaining → close regardless of P&L.
Daily loss limit: 2 consecutive losses → done for the day.
